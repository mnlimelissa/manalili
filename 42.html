"PHOTOGRAPHY CLUB"

PHOTO WALKS

These are casual outings where members explore a specific location and take photos. They can be themed (e.g., nature, architecture, street photography) or open to any subject.




WORKSHOPS

Workshops can cover a wide range of topics, from basic camera settings and composition to advanced techniques like studio lighting or astrophotography.




COMPETITIONS

These events provide an opportunity for members to challenge themselves, learn from others, and receive constructive criticism on their work. Judges can be local professionals or photography instructors.



PHOTO EXHIBITIONS

These events allow members to showcase their best work to a wider audience. It can be a great way to build confidence and promote the club's activities.
